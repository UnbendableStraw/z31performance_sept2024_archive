    There may be some differences as there were some minor changes to the W series engine over the A and B series engines. It had redesigned water galleries for additional cooling and fully floating piston wrist pins.

    Because the OEM water pump for the 84-85 is NLA, I got a Beck/Arnley from RockAuto. Comes with the gasket, and I haven't had any problem with it.

    Unfortunately we can't be too picky about where we get our parts from as Nissan has discontinued many critical parts. I imagine one day most Z31's will look like Mad Max's car from the "Road Warrior."
