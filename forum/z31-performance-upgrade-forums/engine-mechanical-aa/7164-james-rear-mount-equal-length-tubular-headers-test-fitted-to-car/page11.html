    I have run out of materials for the time being, but the next thing to to fabricate a couple of double slip joints to connect the Turbo flange and piping to the 2-1 Y merge.


    Loosely speaking I think the best place for the double slip joint will be between the 2 hold down brackets, the turbo flange will be bolted solid to the old AC M10 bolt holes in the head, And the support brackets will be drilled to 11.5mm to account for thermal expansion ( as per maximum boosts recommendation )

    This should alleviate thermal stress and still provide solid anchoring. Looks like I will need one more 2" v band to go on the end of the 2-1 collector.
