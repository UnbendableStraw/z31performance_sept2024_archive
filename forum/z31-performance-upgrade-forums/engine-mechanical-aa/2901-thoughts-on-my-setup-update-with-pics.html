    Turbos hate the inlet effectâ€¦ any restriction before the turbo needs to be removed. Stick the MAF directly to the compressor, and get rid of that pipe. It looks like a 2.5 or 2.75 inch, so that needs to go.

    Re-rout the intercooler pipe on top of the turbo, but keep it away from the turbine housing. remember that 1 foot of bend is like 6 feet of straight, so keep the piping as simple as you can.

    A bigger turbo would be a good idea. The SAFC is a really bad idea. You will probably end up melting pistons if you use an SAFC. Nistune is more expensive, but it is a lot cheaper and easier than pulling an engine. Also, nistune is a hell of a lot cheaper than a haltech or microtech, and has similar options for it. Have the car tuned by a reputable shop, as inexperienced tuning is the #1 cause of engine failure on a modified turbo car.
