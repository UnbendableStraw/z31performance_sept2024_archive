    I run a Hayden 3653 universal fan controller. This is a basic setup that allows you to place the temp probe in between the fins of the radiator and allows the user to adjust the on/off temp of the fan based on your own preference. I ran the power wire to the ignition switched 12v from the factory A/C fan output from the fuse box.
