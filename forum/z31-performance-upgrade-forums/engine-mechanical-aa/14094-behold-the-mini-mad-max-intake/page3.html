    The B14 cable is waaaaay long enough, I did have to modify it by removing some of the sheathing and re-secure the adjustment section so that I had enough forward cable. You have to remember I'm routing this just below the waste gate so I had to be cautious, no straight shots off the firewall.

    I'll be securing a loop of it to the driver side shock tower column with a 3rd gen maxi bracket, and bringing around to the plenum body where a modified B13 throttle cable adjustment bracket will be mounted. BTW the mounting bracket comes pretty close to the stock distributor, maybe 1/2" away. I'm sure you could get away with a different bracket but the Q45 cable linkages are stretched out from the body it self, so I had that to overcome with sourcing components.

    The firewall mount for the throttle cable appears the same on most Nissan's post '90 to ~97ish making many more styles and lengths available for custom plenum's and to be honest it seems the Maxima cable would have been a better choice, whatevs brah
