    #1 If you value your legs/feet avoid that ACT 6 puck like the plague. Another Z31p member and my self have had catastrophic failures. I'm assure you that's the tip of the iceberg on our platform. I have personally observed failures in other platforms also.

    #2 Set and stay to your budget for the build

    #3 What fuel are you planning to use? E85, pump or race. Consider price, availability, advantages & disadvantages along with the requirements to run each.

    For starters you'll need to address your fuel system, turbo, & intercooler. That's assuming the factory rotating assembly in the VG can take that abuse. For longevity I recommend building a forged assembly. Here's some good videos to watch that may assist you.
