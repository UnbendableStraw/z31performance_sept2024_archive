    There are a couple ways to look at this one could say that there may be a restriction some where causing reduced flow and high pressure which would be really bad. Or maybe you have a rebuilt engine with a reworked oil pump that has been shimmed to tightly causing high pressure. And there are some other causes like running engine honey or oil that is to thick.

    If there is no restriction and oil system is flowing freely I do not see much cause for concern with high oil pressure unless it is so high as to damage the oil filter.
    In my opinion oil flow is much more important than oil pressure so as long as all internal parts are getting the right amount of oil who cares what pressure is.
