    Unless there is something I don't know about Precision turbo's, it has to be water cooled if it is a ball bearing turbo. BB turbo's run oil restrictors in them. For this reason, there is not enough oil flowing through the turbo to actually cool it, just lube it is all. This is why they have to be water cooled.
