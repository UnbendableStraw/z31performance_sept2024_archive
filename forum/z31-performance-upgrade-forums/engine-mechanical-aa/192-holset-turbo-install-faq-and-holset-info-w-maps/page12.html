    Talked with Tim @ Tim's Turbos:

    http://www.ebay.com/itm/Holset-HX35-Tur … 696wt_1396

    He has given me the following information:

    Stock Holset 12cm^2 is close to .78 - .80 A/R
    HE351 has an HX40 comp wheel and a turbine wheel that is smaller than an HX35 wheel and in a smaller 9cm^2 housing. Essentially an HX40/35 hybrid, but smaller on the hotside.

    Seems like a knowledgeable fellow, and perhaps anyone looking for Holset turbos or upgrades can msg him for some info/stock.
