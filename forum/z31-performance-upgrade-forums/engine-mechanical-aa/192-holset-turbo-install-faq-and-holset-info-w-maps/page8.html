    Patience young grasshopper. I dont know the size. I believe its a standard size of around .70 A/R for the compressor housing on the HX40. Housing size does not mean much. The difference between a .60 and .70 A/R is negligable, however, the compressor wheel makes a much bigger difference.

    Oh and to answer your question…NO you cannot swap a HE351 compressor housing onto a HX35. They do not just bolt on, they are different…

    A. Probably attach differently
    B. Different size compressor housing backplate
    C. Different size compressor wheel inducer
    D. Different size compressor wheel exducer

    It will not swap
