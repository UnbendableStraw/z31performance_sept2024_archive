    Posting this here in case anyone is interested. I'll be adding to the imgur album as I go. Car made 567whp on 93 octane pump gas

    Basic Build specs:
    5.3L LM7 out of a 2003 Tahoe, rings gapped
    Summit Stage 2 turbo cam
    PAC 1218 valve springs
    241 heads off a Fbody
    VS Racing billet 7875 Gen 1, 3" exhaust with Vibrant resonator
    LS6 intake, Stock Truck TB
    Z32 transmission with gforce adapter and flywheel
    Z1 Motorsports Z32 TT performance 6 puck clutch
    MS3 Goldbox with 24x harness
    Snake eater 80lb Deka injectors
    Walbro 450 fuel pump
    EFI source 3bar MAP
    Flipped truck manifolds into a hotside I made, Tial V44 wastegate
    2.5" intercooler piping, 13"x24" intercooler, Greddy type RZ BOV
    NGK TR6 spark plugs
    Corvette accessory drive with ICT billet alternator bracket
