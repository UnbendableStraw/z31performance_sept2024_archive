    Injectors are less complicated than they appear

    Replacing the original connector plugs is highly recommended as the original plastic plugs break down and crack.

    Upgrade to the PastPowerPerformance O-ring rails, find a set of R32 skyline injectors, splice in ev-14 connectors, new gaskets… then do yourself a safety solid and convert your fittings to AN so you don't die in a fire.

    before attempting start-up perform multiple leak checks.

    550CC should safely get you over 400WHP, until duty cycle approaches 80% you're safe - this also relevant to you or your tuners comprehension
