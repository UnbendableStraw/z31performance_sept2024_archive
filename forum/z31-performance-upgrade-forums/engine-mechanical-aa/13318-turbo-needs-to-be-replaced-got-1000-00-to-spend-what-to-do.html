    t3 t4 downpipe flange and custom 3" exhaust (or cm exhaust) just a thought… about 400 for the complete turbo (inexpensive ebay turbo, a internal gate flange, original actuator, and a single u bend (3") should be able to get you to the cm part of the exhaust, worst case you need a few feet of 3" straight to connect to it…

    pro's same or faster spool than stock, same boost level as stock so its safe, cooler charge therefore just as safe as stock (since flowing slightly more air at same boost), 3" exhaust meaning more power and quicker spool, go with someone with good feedback, and you shouldn't have a problem… these turbos are reverse enginered from the high dollar turbos so you get the same turbo without the cost of the research to get it there....

    cons people say you didn't spend 4 digits on your turbo....... you have more money in your pocket to worry about what mufler your gonna put on your sexy new 3" exhaust you have to either get a turbo with a 2 inch outlet, or get a few bends to be able to pipe the new turbo in you'l want to buy an intercooler to up the boost, you'll want to spend more money than you have to build even more power....


    whatever you do good luck..... and try not to get too biten by the power bug....
