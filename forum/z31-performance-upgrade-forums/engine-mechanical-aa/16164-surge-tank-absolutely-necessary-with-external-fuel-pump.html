    you can make your own. do you have the original fuel pump? You just need it to fill a smaller cylinder, and have a return line go back to the larger tank return.

    original pump fills small tank.
    high flow pump fills injectors.

    you will not outflow the small pump if it's continuously pumping gas into the small cylinder (surge tank). Just use a high volume low pressure pump.
