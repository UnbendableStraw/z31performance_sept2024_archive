    84-86 are the same length. 87-89 are the same, and 10mm longer than the 84-86. All of the extra length is between the tension rod mount and the ball joint. From tension rod to bushing is the same for all of them. I don't know what the difference is, but the strut tubes are also designed to go with their own style of LCA. Putting 84-86 tubes on 87-89 LCAs will cause negative camber. 87-89 tubes on 84-86 LCAs will cause positive camber (sounds like your issue). This is mentioned about 2/3 down this post. http://z31performance.com/forum/z31-…210#post348210

    I don't know if you noticed, but G-E said those are 87-88 arms, so they would be the same length.
