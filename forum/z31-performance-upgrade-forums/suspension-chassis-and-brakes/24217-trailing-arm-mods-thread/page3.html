    Originally posted by Rick88ss View Post
    Dumb question maybe but what is the advantage of this setup vs the common drilling a hole for camber correction method?
    Don't you need a little negative camber for proper handling?
    Well you eliminate the bind-resisting bushings, so the more you need adjust, the more it helps you avoid tearing things off (or just tearing the bushings)

    The other problem with slotting/drilling, is that by moving the outer pivot down, you move the spring perch down, which means the arm goes back up to meet the spring, and your toe gets worse…

    With this you get full control over camber and toe, without making one or the other worse…
