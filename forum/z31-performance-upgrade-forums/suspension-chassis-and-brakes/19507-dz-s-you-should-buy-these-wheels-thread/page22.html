    frostvectron;349024 wrote: ^ Also agree.

    Adam, good luck with those Gramlights. They looked amazing on your car, imo!

    What's the reason for the double stagger? Not bashing, I just never understood why people did it.
    With adjustable suspension any setup is plausible. I wanted the 9 wides in fronts because that seems to be the ideal fitment with numerous width of tires in a z31. I have full range of everything with my setup, no rubbing, no alignment issues and still maintain a low stance. I wanted wider 18s in the rear to fill the wheel arch and maintain the natural rake the z31s have while keeping zero wheel gap.

    I loved this setup but wanted a fresher look. With the 18x9.5s I have now in the front I needed to pull my fenders, run more camber and narrower tires to maintain full functionality. Compromises the previous setup didnt need.

    Overall the gram lights look awesome and those sizes are pretty z31 conservative in what can be ran. Win win for any prospective buyer. The have over $700 of tires on them.
