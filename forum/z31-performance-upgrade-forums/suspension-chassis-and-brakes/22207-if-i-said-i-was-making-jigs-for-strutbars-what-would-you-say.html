    triangulate the strut bar to the two bolts that hold the headlight bucket down on the center hood latch bar, closer to the radiator opening. that area is boxed in from the headlight bucket area and has some rigidity from the lower radiator support too.

The most common is the simple over the top as the one in my picture it does work efficiently for stock cars, or cars with clearance issues 3/4inch thick 1 1/2 wide.
