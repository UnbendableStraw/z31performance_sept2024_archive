    Originally posted by 84Zed31
    What have you guys done to run the brake lines? My S13 subframe didn't come with the brake lines attached. Did you use the stock Z31 lines and attach it to the new subframe?

    This might have been covered somewhere in this thread but I couldn't find it.
    I made a point to grab the s13 line because I wanted to swap to it being shorter and less complex than the funky z31 setup.

    When I was installing the subframe, I just bent the z31 brake lines out of the way so that I wouldn't deal with them being disconnect and dripping.
    Come to find out that the brake lines used on the z31 are poly armour lines which are bendable without creasing the metal.

    So I ended up using them, bending them roughly to the location that I wanted. From there, I used a SS braided line to the caliper.
    Make sense?
