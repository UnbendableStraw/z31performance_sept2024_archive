    Harness bars…

    01-22-2013, 12:05 AM
    Well guys, I have started making harness bars in the past few weeks. Josh has the first one installed into his car as a test piece. I have changed a few things that he complained about (mounting holes need to have more clearance) and make the bar less of a press fit. I am nearing completion for my personal harness bar, with his recommendations.

    Bars are fully tig welded with SA-80 filler rod. The bar itself is .120 wall DOM tubing. Josh's shipped weighed less than 20 lbs. I can make more, but they are per order and take about a week.

    You will have to remove the stock seat belts in order to install these. So aftermarket harnesses are required. I can get RJS's harnesses at a discount, in any color.
