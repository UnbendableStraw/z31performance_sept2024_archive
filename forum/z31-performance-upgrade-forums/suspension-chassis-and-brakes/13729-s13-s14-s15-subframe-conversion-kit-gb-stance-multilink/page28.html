    Re: S13/S14/S15 Subframe Conversion Kit GB-STANCE MULTILINK!

    You could weld it with a hobbyist machine, like a Miller 165/185. Or the Lincoln that Satan uses, it just means more, smaller passes instead of one large pass. Personally I feel more comfortable with bulding up multiple passes than one big hot pass…you want your weld to penetrate both work pcs evenly. If you were to cut your weld in half the penetration area should look like a circle for T joints, more spade like for butt joints.
