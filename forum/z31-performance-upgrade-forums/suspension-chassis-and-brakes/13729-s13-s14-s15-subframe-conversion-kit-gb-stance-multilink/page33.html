    In comparison most of the AU cars - like mine, are rust free.

    All my welding is now done, just need to clean it all up next weekend.
    I also ended up buying some more 3" metal sheets and rather than cutting the sheet the 2X bolts weld onto, I went all across with 1 piece and backed this with 4 other sheets that stepped out in length.
    This way I get to use the old diff anchor points for additional strength/anchoring of amended structure.
    Just had to shorten one of the petrol tank supports which was easy.
    I also had custom bushes made for the top of subframe all round giving me another 7mm clearance so less rail intrusion.
