    Hey guys, I just found this in a for sale thread on another forum, and thought you guys might be interested. (I've omitted some stuff like the prices and whatnot for the seller's sake)

    "ISIS S13 coilovers with stance 8/6 springs and stance lower adjustment

    These are BRAND NEW ISIS coilovers still have the original box just took them out to swap over the springs and collars to stance. This will make the coils go MUCH MUCH lower than regular ISIS stuff will do.








    So basically, what I'm reading this as, is that the Isis coilovers have the same thread pitch as Stance coilovers so they could, theoretically, fit into the S9/Stance adaptors for the fronts of our cars, and the rears would go in the same way as the Stance do.

    now, you may be wondering why this is interesting? well Isis coilovers sell for less than $700 (befoar shipping) from Enjuku Racing, which is moar than $400 cheaper than the cheapest Stance coils.

    Now, I'm not in any way trying to make it sound like cheaper is bettar, but some guys don't have the budget for super nice coilovers (like me )
