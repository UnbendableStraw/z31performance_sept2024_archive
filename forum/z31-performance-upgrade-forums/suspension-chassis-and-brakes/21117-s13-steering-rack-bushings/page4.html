    So here's an update on my Passenger's Side Solid Steering Rack Mounts: I've got one customer whose rack is apparently moving in the Delrin mount. He's running 265/35/18 Toyo Proxy R1R front tires and is running a power rack with no pump. These mounts are in about 20 other Z31s at this point and my original S12 version is in about 50 cars without a single issue. The rack in my S12 hasn't moved at all in 3+ years of hard use on sticky tires. For reference, the Z31 rack is 1mm smaller diameter than an S12 rack but the mounts are made from the same material with the same amount of 'crush' when the bolts are fully tight.

    Also at issue is the fact that there were apparently two widths of Z31 rack mounting straps even though Nissan says they were all the same. My solid mounts are made for the wider brackets that apparently came on the earlier cars. (I don't know when they changed.) The guy with the slipping rack says he has the wide mounting strap that fits properly in the bushing. From reading above that the poly mounts have to be glued to the rack to keep it from slipping perhaps we'll just need to do the same with solid plastic mounts if they slip.

    I do intend to produce driver's side Delrin rack mounts at some point but people with S12s have been running these on the passenger's side only for nearly 10 years (someone else produced them long ago, but stole a bunch of people's money and vanished) and there are no complaints on the S12 forum. Actually everybody that has one, mine or the original guy's, raves about it.

    So that's where things are at. I have the mounts in stock if anybody wants to buy one. They're $30 plus $7 shipping in the USA. I ship worldwide at additional expense.
