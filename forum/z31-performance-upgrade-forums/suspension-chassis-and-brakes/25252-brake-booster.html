    This is what ive got going on


    I went to an S14 Short CLutch MC

    Z31 LHD BMC ( because of my RHD CAR ) this gave me braided brake line tails i could route away from the engine not towards it.

    This also let me build a really nice BMC heat Shield.

    The 280zx booster is wider and flatter and is sized correctly for the Z31 stock BMC in my particualr case it had a cut away in the right spot to give me clearance i needed.

    You need to build a 4 bolt adaptor plate or have it taken apart and the studs reloacted.








    Don't think to hard about it, just make stuff

    It looks like a RHD BMC and braided lines could really help your clearance.
