    are you even sure you're looking at the right transmission in your car?

    How can the dealership screw something like the seal up. I just looked at nissan FAST and it's exactly as the FICHE shows and you get 4 options when you enter no engine/trans details, and you get two options when you enter both engine, trans, and 05/85 as the production date.
