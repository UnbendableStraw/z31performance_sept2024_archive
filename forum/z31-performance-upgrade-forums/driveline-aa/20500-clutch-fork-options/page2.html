    Careless;319652 wrote: the difference is dave advertises the clutch as a modified z33 pressure plate.
    That's only for my 350z lower staged stuff.. all the Z31 and Z33 SS (aka stage 3) are a 350z pressure plate that is not available from Nissan. They average around 2700lbs of clamp load with no modification. The 'stock' Exedy 350z pressure plates are 1790lbs, my standard modified Exedy is 1910lbs and a blown out Exedy is around 2300lbs. That being said, I have modified the SS pressure plates up to 3800lbs on a 'blown out' version and have held over 800tq.
