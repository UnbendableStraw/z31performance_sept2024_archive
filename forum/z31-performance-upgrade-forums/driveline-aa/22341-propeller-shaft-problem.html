    thats considered the drive shaft. US Z31's never came with a 2 piece shaft - no center bearing. you need to get under the car, verify Universal joint condition - at both ends of the shaft. trans mount, and rear diff mount would be wise to check too. Lastly - check the rear differential backlash/rotational play. I slightly remember my 87T - with the clutch style LSD unit would clunk/chatter when on/off the throttle or shifting quickly.
