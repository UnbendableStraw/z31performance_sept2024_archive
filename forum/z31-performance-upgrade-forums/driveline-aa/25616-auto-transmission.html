    Hey man,

    The level 10 mods are not exactly secret sauce. If you take the car to any transmission shop that does muscle or drag cars they will be able to set you up with the same or very similar for not much more than a stock rebuild (usually seems to run just over a grand, but expect it to take a couple weeks while they wait for parts.) That's what I did, got a two-year warranty off of it too.

    In fact, the place that did mine said that unless you are putting down 300+rwtq and drive really hard they'd just do a stock rebuild and a cooler and call it a day.
