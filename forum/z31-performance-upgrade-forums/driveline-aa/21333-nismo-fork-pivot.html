    85NA2T;327956 wrote: Hmmm thats odd.I know the fork wont work but my pivot was the same? Idk
    The reason being is that the fork upgrade needs a shorter pivot ball to work. It seems that Nissan used a shorter pivot ball for the 71c transmissions and 2004+ 350z. A longer one was used for the 30a and 2003 350z transmissions.

    Pic I found:



    This pdf about the Nismo pivot balls shows the short to be 41mm long vs the long ones 54mm:
    http://www.nismo.co.jp/en/products/c…/pdf/pivot.pdf
