    you dont need to enlarge the threaded holes in the Z31 diff housing.

    you just need to get 10mm to 8mm hole spacers/sleeves from mcmaster-carr so that you can reuse your 8mm bolts.
    it's the same thing people use when they go from 12mm holes in their R200 Ring gears when bolting it to the 10mm holes on the r200 differential carrier.
