    comrade_charlie wrote: is the Z t5 a wc or nwc? Whats the difference? syncros?
    Nissan T-5 is NWC.
    WC T-5's have needle bearings under the gears instead of just using oil. They have different end bearings and the syncros are fiber instead of brass except for fifth which is the same as the NWC. Also the gears may be a bit heaver in the Mustang set up.
