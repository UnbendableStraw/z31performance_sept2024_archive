    Originally posted by 510_Z31 View Post
    When I bought that kit I had trouble getting the trans into 5th and reverse. I believe the pivot point on those arm are too long for the 30a.
    I took what you said and applied it.

    I punched a shim washer from 18ga (~1mm) aluminum with a 49.5mm (1.96") OD and 38.1mm (1.5") ID. (I have a Rotex 18-A turret punch at work)
    I installed it beneath the stock wave spring washer to raise the ebay short throw shifter pivot point by 1mm.

    I can now easily shift into 5th and reverse.

    I discovered that by forcing the transmission into 5th and reverse (with the clamp assembly fully tightened and the cup bushing in the correct orientation)
    it was actually shredding bottom of the nylon bushing.

    With the shim washer installed and the clamp assembly tightened, it no longer hangs up on 5th and reverse.

    I'm not sure how much this pivot offset diminishes the benefit of a short throw shifter. Probably not much.

    If i can locate the original document, i'll update my writeup.
