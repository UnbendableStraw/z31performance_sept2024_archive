    While these all have the same bell housings to bolt up to the vg30/33, nothing else will work in terms of bolt on.
    You will need a custom drive shaft and the shift hole made larger in your cabin.

    These transmissions are no where near the same as the z31 30a.

    On top of that all, the gearing ratios on these are truck oriented.
