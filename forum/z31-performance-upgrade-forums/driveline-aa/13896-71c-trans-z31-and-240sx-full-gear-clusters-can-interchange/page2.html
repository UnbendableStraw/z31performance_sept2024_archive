    Bell housing swap:

    Slaves from z31 and s13 are nearly identical. Bolt spacing is the same. I've bolted BOTH up without issue.

    240sx FRONT SEAL PLATE has the pivot ball in the wrong spot for the zx slave. You have to use the front plate that came with the 71c in your zx.

    RB20 71c's have the same pattern and bolt up to the bellhousing.
