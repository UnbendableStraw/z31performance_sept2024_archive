    FYI - don't use the crappy bushing that comes with the shifter. Spend the extra $10 and buy a new nissan one. To get it on set the bushing on something softer than concrete (wood, plastic, etc) then align the shifter pivot in there and hit it down with a rubber mallet. Should pop right on with one good tap. If you are really strapped for cash use the one on the stock shifter, but I like the ability to switch back to the stock shifter if I ever need to.
