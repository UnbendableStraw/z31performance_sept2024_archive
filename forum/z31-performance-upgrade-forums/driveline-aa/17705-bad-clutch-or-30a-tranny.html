    Bad clutch or 30a tranny?

    04-25-2012, 11:26 PM
    So I am working on my Shiro.
    The car has 95k miles.

    The problem is it grinds while shifting every gear.

    What I have done so far....

    Bypassed clutch damper with 280zx hose.
    Bled entire clutch system with new fluid.
    Adjust clutch pedal rod.
    The master and slave have no sign of leakage. Both parts seem to be new have the Nissan logo stamp, OEM.
    Drained and refilled the tranny with new fluid.
    Replaced shifter bushing with new OEM.

    The reason why I am asking this question is that I am not 100% sure its the tranny's syncros.
    Generally when a tranny starts to grind, its 1 or maybe 2 gears (depending on syncro) that grinds.
    This is EVERY gear.
    Which makes me think that the clutch is old, worn, and not properly disengaging.

    I did a little googling and found that it IS possible for a bad clutch to do this.


    What are your thoughts or suggestions?

    I also want to add that the tranny is making a whine that relates to the RPMs. This would tell me worn gearing or somethingâ€¦
