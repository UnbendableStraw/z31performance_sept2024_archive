    You dont need trans mounts, starter, or flywheel/clutch. Just the trans and driveshaft. The start is the same for all Z31s, 30A, T5, 71C or Automatic. And the trans mounts and crossmember interchange on all manuals. Annnnd....obviously, the shifter will be in the transmissionâ€¦shift knob threads are the same for all except very very early 84s. Some people say the 250mm clutches wont clear the 71C bellhousing, but I've never had an issue. If you do, just hit it with a grinder a tiny bit because interference will be minimal.
