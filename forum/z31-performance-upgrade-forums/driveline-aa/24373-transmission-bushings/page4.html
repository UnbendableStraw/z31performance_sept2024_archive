    Originally posted by Reds89 View Post
    Received mine last week, thank you Gary. One simple question, do these require the bushing grease like Prothane control arm bushings do?
    Since there is no movement of the bushing or part within it that moves no grease should be required. There is a thin coating of silicone mold release that is on the surface at the time of manufacture. It helps a bit with installation. If the inside surface is bare metal then a thin application of silicone grease to the metal surface the bushings are inserted into will help reduce rusting.
