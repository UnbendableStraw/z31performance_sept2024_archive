    Mine was from a Z32NA.
    The Z31 crown is 5/16 thicker than the Z32NA's, so when the Z31 crown is bolted to the Z32 VLSD pumpkin, the assembly is moved 5/16 to left side of the diff housing. The shims are left in the same location but the spacer is now swapped to the right side of the diff housing. So the left axle stub, the one with the clip is unseated 5/16 and must be modified to sit flush with the diff housing as it should.
