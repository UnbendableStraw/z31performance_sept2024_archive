    So? Take a look at most transmissions. A bike is a good example. The older bikes (say 1970s CB750) have massive gears, very very thick and heavy, but the engines only make about 67 horsepower. Then there's the new bikes, an example would be a Kawi litre bike. They have very very thin gears in the transmission, yet are making about 197 horse. The gears are made differently to allow it to be stronger and not just strip when you dump the clutch at 9k. Simple as that. Material and treatment.
