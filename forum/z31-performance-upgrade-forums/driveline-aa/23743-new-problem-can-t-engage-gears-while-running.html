    When I bought my Z, this is the reason the previous owner stopped driving it. He couldn't get it in gear while running, but it was just a bad clutch master cyl.

    When it's in first gear start it and get on a level surface and try and see if the car wants to pull forward with the clutch fully disengaged (pedal pushed). That made it pretty obvious for me. Wore out the throwout bearing VERY fast though, so if that is the problem be careful limping it around unless you feel like dropping the trans any time soon.
