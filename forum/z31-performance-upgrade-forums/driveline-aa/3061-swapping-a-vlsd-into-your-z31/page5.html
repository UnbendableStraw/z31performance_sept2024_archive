    frostvectron;330303 wrote: Does anyone know if the tripod from a NA z31 axle has the same spline as the inner race on the z31 turbo axle?
    Yes, all years

    Turbo uses different kind of joints and thicker shaft, they use the birfield (caged balls), the non-turbo uses a tripod (exposed rollers)

    If you try hard enough it might be possible to find compatible birfield parts to fit the non-turbo shaft, but don't think anyone tried
