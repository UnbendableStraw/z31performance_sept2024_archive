    remove the sway bar and drop the driveshaft, check to see if the pinion nut backed it self out some how- perhaps someone who serviced it last time forgot to stake the nut to prevent it from walking. the torque applied to the nut may have finally given up and it's now loosening up and causing excessive play. if that's not the case, before diving into it… I would un-stake it and tighten it up a bit and re-stake. play with some depth settings.

    might as well do that before you go about removing the whole thing.
