    Caught the message on the answering machine, and the post on z31.com all at the same time… 8)
    VG30E(T) FlyWheel Bolts Are M10 x 25mm x 1.0 ( qty. of 6 )
    VG30E(T) Automatic Tranny flexplate Bolts are M10 x 20mm x 1.0
    If replacing these with "non-oem hardware"…these are the specs to chase down…(and be prepared to spend some $$$)

    The minimum standards the bolts should be replaced with:
    ~Minimum Tensile Strength: 180,000psi.
    ~Minimum Shear Strength: 108,000psi.
    *In other words…grade 12.9*


    The Pressure Plate to Flywheel Hardware( qty. of 6 )- M8 x 15mm x 1.5 + lockwasher(grade8 acceptable, grade 10.9 recomended)
