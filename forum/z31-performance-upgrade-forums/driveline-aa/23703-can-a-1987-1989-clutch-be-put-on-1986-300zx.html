    Just to set the facts straight here…

    The bolt patterns for the pressure plate between the 240mm and 250mm are different, they are not the same.
    So you cannot bolt a 240mm clutch setup onto a 250mm flywheel
    Or you cannot bolt a 250mm clutch setup onto a 240mm flywheel

    Also, only the 30a transmission accepts a 250mm. Its the only bell housing that has enough clearance.
    All other bell housings will have to have material removed from the inside to fit.


    On a side note, I run a stage 5 DXD clutch setup. It is a 240mm clutch onto a Fidanza flywheel.
