    I would say swap diff covers so it bolts up. As far as it being two or four pinion, I don't know. I would guess two since it is an open diff, but I guess there are some open diffs that are 4 pinion. Speedo cable… dunno. Companion flange dunno. Man, there is a lot I don't know about this. Here is what I do know…

    You can pull the ring and pinion out of it and put it on your LSD z31 diff. Then everything would work, minus the speedo.

    Or, you can swap companion flanges and the diff cover so it bolts right up (assuming the cross member bolt pattern is the same which I'm sure it is). I am willing to place money that it is the typical 29 spline input shafts as well.

    Desireable… I guess SORTA desireable? I mean, it is probably still an open diff. The only reason someone would want it is simply because of the drag racing gear ratio. For that, you could sell just the ring and pinion for the same price as the whole diff I would imagine. Honestly, If I were looking for one, the MOST I would be willing to pay for the ring and pinion is $200. I am willing to splurge more than most on a z31 though.
