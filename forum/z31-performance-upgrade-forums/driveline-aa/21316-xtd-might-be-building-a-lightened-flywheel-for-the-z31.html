    XTD might be building a lightened flywheel for the z31!

    04-22-2013, 12:34 AM
    The company that makes this:

    http://www.ebay.com/itm/XTD-PRO-LITE…230b9d&vxp=mtr

    Might be making an inexpensive flywheel for the z31! I emailed them after noticing they offer a lightened flywheel for just about every other Nissan product except ours. They offer several clutches, but no flywheel. I made a case, and directed them to this website so they could see how dedicated z31 owners are to our cars, and they sent me an email back saying they may try to develop one this fall! I'm sure plenty of people will say bad things about this company, and talk about how much better all the other flywheels are. I'd even agree with them in some ways. For people who know what's up, and want a top-notch product, ASCO flywheels and Fidanza flywheels are still going to be around. But for someone who wants to spend less than $400 on a flywheel, it would be nice to have an alternative. I say any business that wants to invest in these cars is welcome!

    Opinions? I'm kinda excited, as long as they aren't yanking my chain!
