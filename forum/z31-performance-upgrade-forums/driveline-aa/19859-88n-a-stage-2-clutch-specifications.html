    easy! If you're basically a stock car looking for a heavy duty version of a stock clutch, the stage 1 HD would work very well. Holds 300tq and runs $225. If you're interested in something a little stronger, that can hold a little more power or abuse, the stage 2 Daily is not much more at $265 and holds 315tq. The difference is the friction material. The stage 1 is a stock pressure plate with upgraded friction material, the stage 2 daily is a modified pressure plate with the same upgraded friction material. The modified pressure plate is generally around 100-150lbs of extra clamping force over stock. Of course, more clamping on the disc results in more torque holding.
