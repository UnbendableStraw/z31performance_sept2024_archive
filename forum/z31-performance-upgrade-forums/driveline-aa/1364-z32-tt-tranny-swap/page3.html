    I have not had time to read through all these post but the way you mounted the shifter to the body will quickly take out the u-joint on the transmissions shifter linkage. The stress from the trans moving against the shifter mounted directly to the body kills them pretty fast. You have to have a SOLID trans mount so it can't move against the body if you are going to do it that way. Just a heads up

    I have a solid shifter mount sort of like mazworks but is much beefier and adjustable so you can get the shifter in the correct position.



    I also have a shift boot plate for the Z32 that may well be adapted into the Z31 also.
