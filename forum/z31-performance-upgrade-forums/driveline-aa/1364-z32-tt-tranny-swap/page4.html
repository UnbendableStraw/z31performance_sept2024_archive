    my driveshaft solution was unique yet fairly simple.
    i stole my pathfinders rear drive shaft, found a yoke that bolts to the r200 differential flange and has 1310 ujoints to match the pathfinders, trim the collar on the slip yoke to fit in the cars transmission slip yoke cup, shorten it to length, and voila.
    stronger than stock one piece ds with bigger stronger ujoints done for less than 100 bucks. cant beat that.

    i had to make a custom tranny crossmember which turned out really good i think and incorporates the stock z32 tranny mount.

    any R30a bellhousing wth the starter location that bolts to the block, and matching slave cylinder with banjo bolt hose.
