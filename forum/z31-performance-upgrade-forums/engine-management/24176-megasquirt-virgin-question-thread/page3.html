    It's quite easy to get a vg going with a megasquirt.
    Im using the MS2 3.57 in my 88T, 500cc inj, hy35 on 20psi, LC1 using the autotune feature( worth every dollar) i basically pulled all the engine bay wiring, then ran 3 new relays and a independant fuse panel just for the MS.Im using the factory trigger wheel. I cut the pigtails for the tps and cas for easy wiring.everything works normally except for you must wire the tach and the single wire for your dash temp gauge.
    there are some diagrams online that can help with the wiring. if you need help with your settings please let me know.
