    Bee*R Rev Limiter Wire up.

    10-07-2012, 04:54 AM
    Hey guys, I bought a Used Bee*R unit from a guy on ebay the other day, and through countless searches and attempts, I have finally figured out the configuration if anyone would like to know or correct me if im wrong!!

    Bee*R wireset

    Red = 12V (Accessory for stereo)
    Black = Ground (Ground for ecu)
    Yellow = Rpm signal - I used pin 17 (G/B) which is the 120 deg C.A.S.
    Green = Ignition signal - I used pin 8 (G/Y) which is the 1 deg C.A.S.
    White = 2 step switch (Ground signal) Can be wired to ebrake or regular switch that grounds out signal.

    At first, I had the green wire to the 120 deg and the yellow to the 1 deg, but it would not start. This is the basic setup for our Nissans in general. But as the result of a lot of researching, I found that if it does not work with that setup up, usually it mean the green and yellow Bee*R wires are backwards, and BOOM works like a charm id say.

    Another point!!! I believe that since our vehicles are single coil, we have to cut and insulate the Gray loop wire as if we were setting up the unit for a 4 cylinder. Normally for 6 cylinder engines, you would leave it intact. But this unit is mainly used for coil pack ignition systems. The brown loop wire needs to remain intact. If anyone has any more information on this or see's any flaws with my setup please let me know! Hope this helps out my Z fam out there!
