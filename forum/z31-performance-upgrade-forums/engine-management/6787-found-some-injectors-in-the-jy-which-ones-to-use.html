    Go to witchhunter.com and look up injector flow ratings using the part numbers on the side of the fuel injectors.

    You will need to have those junkyard fuel injectors cleaned and flowtested before use. From all the Mercedes Benz and other fuel injectors that I have pulled from the junkyard, all of them were at least 10% - 20% below their rated flow values before cleaning.
