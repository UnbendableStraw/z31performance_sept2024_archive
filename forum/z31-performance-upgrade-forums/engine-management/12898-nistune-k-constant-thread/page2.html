    I wouldn't go by manufacturer when tuning the latency. Bosch makes many different kinds of injectors and the coils inside the injector body may be different and may require more or less adjustment- and I'm sure there will be one or two cases where you might even see the same injector have different latencies that work for either person using them.

    I think this should only be used as a starting point- and the more data we have (even if there are two which are the same injector), the more people can try and not just shoot blindly at getting their injectors to be properly operated by the ECU.
