    You could put Nistune in any of the above ECUs and be fine. You'll want to match your ECU up to the injectors you're planning to use, so you need to find out if they are going to be high or low impedance. 84-87 ECUs are low, 88-89 are high. 88-89 are also already socketed, so they are a little easier to do the install, but if you send your ECU out to someone like Autovaugh they'll do the Nistune install for an extra $50. I'd get in touch with them when you're ready to purchase. Also, go here and read read read http://nistune.com/
