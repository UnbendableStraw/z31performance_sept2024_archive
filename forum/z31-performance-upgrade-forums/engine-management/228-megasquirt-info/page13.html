    Red wire is +12v source, can come from anywhere. Either on the MS unit itself or in the wiring harness somewhere. You can even use the +12v wire that goes to the CAS itself.

    You attach one end of the resistor to the +12v wire, the other to the signal. The signal wire still continues to the MS box.
