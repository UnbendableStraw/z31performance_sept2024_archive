    A RX7 Collent temp sensor will screw into the factor Intake (The reason i used) Here are the calibrations for a Rx7 and Rx8.




    Rx8:The rx-8 Fsm states it's coolant sensor as
    20C(68*f) 2.2k-2.7k
    80C(176*f) 287-239
    91C(196*f) 227-240

    Rx7:
    -20c (-4f) 16.2K = 16200
    20c (68f) 2.5K = 2500
    80c (176f) 0.3K = 300
