    just for comparison:

    hx35 is similar to the he351 hybrid turbo I have on my car, except my comp wheel is 4mm larger.

    at your 13psi, i can almost guarantee you that it should be making more than 270whp (but since I commented on TJ's build that his numbers were slightly lower than usual, people didn't like hearing it and got sand in their vaginas over it- I am not saying it's 100% fact).

    My neighbour has an s13 with an SR20 @ 14psi or so, and I've experienced and played with his car quite a couple of times… Enough to experience and gauge improvements we make to the vehicle like small boost leak fixes or exhaust valve cutouts and such. It's a solid 250-260whp vehicle at that boost level with the recent small mods made.

    And my car would shit all over his car at 9-10psi, and our z31's are slightly heavier.

    You either have a serious boost leak, serious blow by, or serious timing issues. If he's dyno tuned it and said it's close to ready- I doubt it's a fuel issue, your AFR's might be "tuned" properly… but the timing might be way low and not being taken advantage of. This is something a tuner would normally do on the first couple of runs anyways… They play with timing usually after fuel.
