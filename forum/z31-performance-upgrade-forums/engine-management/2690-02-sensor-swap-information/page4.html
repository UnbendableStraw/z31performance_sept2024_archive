    I've been looking at the wiring diagram, on an 85 na going to 87t ecu… It looks like the yellow pin 115 and black pin 36 are identical. The only change appears to be in L in the 85, which after tracing it looks to go to the fuel pump. On the 87t diagram the L wire at the o2 is now a G/w wire that runs to pin 34, sharing the ignition coil now instead of fuel pump
