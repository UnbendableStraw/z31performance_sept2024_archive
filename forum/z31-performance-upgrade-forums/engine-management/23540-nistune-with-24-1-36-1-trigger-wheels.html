    I don't think you understand the side effects of trying to do what I think you're thinking about…

    The M30 ecu is sequential up to a point, and can be made to work perfectly on z31's vg30, but the z31 ecu is a bit slow to process things, so jecs chose to make it batchfire as a way to save "work" like many other ecus of the time… it only has 2 drivers, regardless how often it fires, you'd need extra electronics to pick which injector does the job

    Ignition is already sequential, you'd have to switch to a distributorless wasted spark setup, maybe a separately controlled msd/crane ignition box, it's possible to get that aspect going relatively easily, the injection will be next to impossible without a ton of extra electronics

    You'd be better off going standalone
