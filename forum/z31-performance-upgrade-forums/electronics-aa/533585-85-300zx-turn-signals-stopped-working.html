    Just off the top, I'm thinking contacts in your signal stalk off the steering wheel. Perhaps removing it and examining the contacts will show something. They may need cleaning or maybe something is broken. Of course you will have to remove the steering wheel first, but before you tug on it place a pillow between it and your face. And don't forget to mark the spline (magic marker or white out) so you can insert the wheel in its original position.
