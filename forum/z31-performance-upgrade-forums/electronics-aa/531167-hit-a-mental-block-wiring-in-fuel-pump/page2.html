    ECM 108 is a GROUND signal, so you have to supply power to pin 85 in order to activate the relay. You can connect the original power wire that used to go to the pump to pin 85. The way I wired it, I took the two wires (Black and Blue/White) coming from the 3-pin connector that originally fed the fuel pump and connected them to 85 and 86 on my relay. Then connect your dedicated 12V battery source to Pin 30 and pump positive to Pin 87. Lastly, ground the pump to chassis.
