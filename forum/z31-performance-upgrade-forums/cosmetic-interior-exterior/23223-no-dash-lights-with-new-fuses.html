    Headlight switch could easily be the culprit. It turns on the headlights, tail lights, side marker lights, dash lights, anything that comes on when the headlights come on. So yeah, get rid of or clean the headlight/blinker switch. A very common problem.


    A link to help you clean the switch.

    http://88hybrid.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=57&t=13202

    A couple of links to help you with the removal of the switch.

    http://xenonzcar.com/z31/Headlamps.html

    http://zhome.com/ZCMnL/84HowTo/84hlswitch.html
    Restore it, Don't crush it. They don't make them like this anymore.
