    Blue carpet with brown headliner? That doesn't seem right. If the carpet is in otherwise good shape you can go over it all with carpet and upholstery cleaner, remove it if you'd like to get the best results, spray it down, follow directions, there's a few variation, let it air out outside the car and reinstall it. Rear carpet will be the easy one as it has I believe 4 plastic clips that can be removed from the storage compartment cover, remove the bar, and it'll come right out and even lay flat. If it is beat up check black dragon and MSA, but call or E-mail both, if it's low inventory and being discontinued they won't list it in the catalog. Also check here, http://www.autocarpetdeals.com/Merch…ry_Code=NISSA3 I'm sure you'll be replacing the headliner as the smoke probably went right up to that, I'd go with standard headliner material as it's foam backed and will set into the trim better. As for floor mats, I've never tried Z33 mats but I have Z32 mats and while they are not perfect they are damn nice. There are a number of places to find them, E-bay is probably the easy way, but keep in mind they are not molded, they are flat and have stitching all the way around to keep it from unraveling and for looks, meaning if you wanted someone could measure your floor, cut any carpet you want, back it with rubber, (hopefully with all those spikes to hold it in place) and sew around it with the proper stitching. Unless you want something custom or can't find what you're looking for, that is really more work than is needed when they are otherwise in many locations, I believe even JC Whitney had some, but I don't really even look at them much anymore.
