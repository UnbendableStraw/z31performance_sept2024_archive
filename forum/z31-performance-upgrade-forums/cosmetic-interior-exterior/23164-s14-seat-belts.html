    S14 Seat belts

    01-05-2014, 09:56 PM
    After installing my Z32 seats I noticed my seat belts were too short. Once I was buckled in I only had a few inches of play left in the belt before it hit the end. Barely enough to reach the drivers side floor, and not even close for the passenger side. I looked into other options and found the design of the S14 belts to be very similar to the Z31. I bought a set and found the belt to be 12-18" longer than the Z31 (didn't take an exact measurement) and the set I had also matched with the Z31 buckles. They were pretty much a bolt in install. I couldn't use the side with the small screws, but they don't really seem important anyway, and I had to grind off a small hooked bit that was just for positioning. I used the bolts that came out of the car on the main reel part, and the upper part behind the panel, then used the S14 bolt at the bottom behind the seat. The main reel part doesn't sit quite as far back in the hole as the Z31 belts did, but they fit fine behind the panel, so I see no issue there. I also had to cut off a plastic guide that was on the belt because there isn't really a place for it on the Z. The new one's are much better, with plenty of slack for moving around.
