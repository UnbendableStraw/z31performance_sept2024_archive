    The original intent of the rear louvre was for security .. Keeping contents of the rear hatch covered. I don't believe the rear shelf shade was standard for all models and window tinting was not as popular as it is today. The rear louvers also did not significantly affect rear view visibility (unlike aggressive tinting)

    Side louvers .. Offered additional security for the hatch area.

    Headlamp louvers .. Offered headlamp protection (I got 'em because I was running Cibie Z-Beams (early lamps that allowed bulb replacements) .. and were an alternative to the plastic covers.

    IMO ..
    You can run .. The rear louvers by themselves and "look" OK
    You can run .. Rear/side and Rear/headlamp (I'm biased) combinations and "look" OK
