    Yeah, I got free samples from these guys about a year ago. Even their standard pile seems ahead of the stock Z31 carpeting in quality, though we're comparing brand new to 26-year-old so that might not be fair. Their ultra-plush is really really nice, like what you'd find on an S-class Benz. Some people have had complaints about the hatch carpet fitment (I think their original template was a 20-year-old hatch carpet that had shrunk over the years) so you might want to ask them about that before you buy. There are at least a couple people on here that have bought carpets from these guys. Here's a link to one of the threads; there are links to other carpeting threads within the posts:
