    knock sensor or detonation sensor?

    if its a vg33 block the knock sensor is under the lower intake manifold under the water pipes in the block and its a 2 pin plug.

    that plug looks like it goes right by the oil pressure sending unit detonation sensor on the stock motor…

    did you get that alternator tested before you put it in?

    check the plug that goes into the alternator, one of those wires (check fsm) is the sense wire and if its not connected or plugged in correctly the atlernator will not charge.
