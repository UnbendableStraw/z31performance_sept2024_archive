    And you won't find anything on this topic strangely. I did this conversion a couple months ago so here's what your gonna need.

    - 87 to 89 main cluster
    - 87 to 89 pod gauges
    - 87 to 89 dash wiring harness
    - 87 to 89 analog speed sensor
    - Analog fuel level sender
    - Analog Oil Pressure sender

    The speed sensor is actually very easy to install. You'll need the find the factory connector that went to the digital speed sensor by the battery and splice two wires
    to the analog gauge harness. Thats it it's pretty simple. I'll take a pic of my setup so you can see which wires go where. I should probably do a write up on this since
    no one else seems to want to.
