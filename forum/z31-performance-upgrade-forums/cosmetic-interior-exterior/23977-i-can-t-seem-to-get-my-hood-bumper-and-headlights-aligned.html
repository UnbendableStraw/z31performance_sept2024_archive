    If you loosen the 4 hood bolts (2 each side), then barely, I mean barely tighten them to where they barely hold, you can now play with the alignment.
    Once you have it, very carefully lift the hood up and tighten the bolts down all the way.

    Its always always helps to have a buddy helping you on this one.
