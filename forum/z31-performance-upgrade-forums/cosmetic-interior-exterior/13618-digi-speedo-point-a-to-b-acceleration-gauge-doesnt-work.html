    Maybe.

    I'm not sure if the Maxi sensor is correct signal wise for the Z.

    Easiest way to tell if the speedo cable spins is unplug the sensor from the cable, jack up the rear wheels, and start engine and put it in 3rd gear and bring up the speed and look at the cable. If it doesnt spin, then the cable is broke or the drive gear on the end of the speedo is stripped. In rare cases the gear on the output shaft inside the trans is stripped or broken.
