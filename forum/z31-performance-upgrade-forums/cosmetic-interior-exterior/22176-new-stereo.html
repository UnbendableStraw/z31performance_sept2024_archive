    I would just run new wire to all four speakers. At least my car used a high level common ground setup, which new stereos CANNOT interface. Just run new wire through the door boots (I used a coat hanger and carefully pulled the new wire through. It will suck to do, and take time, but is worth it!) and hook it up to power. I used a small speaker to retain the bitching betty.
