    There are two headlight "standards" if I recall correctly, ECE and DOT. The noticeable difference is that ECE headlights have a reversed step in the beam. In the USA, DOT (LHD) headlamps have a step that goes from the left and up to right. This lowers the beam on the side of oncoming traffic. ECE (RHD) headlamps the step goes from the right up to left since they drive on the opposite side of the road.

    Not sure about inspection standards, but you might fail since they test the headlights.
