    I ran into a very similar situation years ago trying to convert an 84 Digital dash to 87 analog clusters. I started to slowly get things like rpm, temp, fuel, etc working but its required to change sensors/sending units where applicable. To my knowledge the conversions are only successful when swapping entire dashharnesses and from the same year. There are wiring differences between the years.

    Its possible, but be prepared to use the FSM and retrace the leads or create new ones.
