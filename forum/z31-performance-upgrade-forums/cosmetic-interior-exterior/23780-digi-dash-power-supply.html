    Here is a small sample of what you need

    Direction sensor installed in the roof for the compass. Compass direction computer installed by spare tire in hatch. Digital fuel tank float. Different Speedo cable to go with the speed sensor. Different main body harness different instrument harness. Different radio sub harness. gll radio amplifier under the passenger seat. Different steering wheel. Different steering wheel plastic cover different backlighting behind the dash switches (green instead of orange). Steering wheel switch receiver.
