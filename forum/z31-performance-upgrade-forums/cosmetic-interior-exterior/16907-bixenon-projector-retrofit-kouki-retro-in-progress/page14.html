    MagicZ31;341036 wrote: Is there any warranty for your items?
    MagicZ31;341118 wrote:     I mean if we buy his item and it comes broken or if something happens..
    The warranty is kinda hard with custom pieces. Everyone will go through different climates and different situations which makes it hard to give a warranty. At the moment im putting the purchase of the projectors on the buyer, that way if you have any issues with the projectors itself it will be warrantied through you and the company, and not me. I can only do so much in making sure the unit works correctly and that its installed.

    The lenses themselves that are made by me should not leak. I always soak them in water to see if water gets through. Fogging though will happen, and I have done a shit load of testing to eliminate that issue, but it will still happen. The alignment wont be spot on, but all you have to do is rotate the projector a millimeter or so. I try my best to align them to eachother on a bench.

    IF the unit comes broken, I would want complete honesty and need pics. I try and package them very good. This is what insurance is for from UPS.
