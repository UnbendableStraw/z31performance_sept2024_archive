    Re: 7x6 bixenon Projector Retrofit

    Prototype number 2!

    Since I had a clearence issue, I knew there had to be another way to secure the projector. So decided to work on this glass set today (and it fits perfectly by the way). These are glass lenses and there were a BIOTCH to open. The plastic lens opened at 275 degrees for 7 min. These ones was 375 for 20 minutes!!! The housing actually warped a little bit, which helped pry off the glue…but when it cooled down it formed back to normal.

    I now have no more issue with that bar behind the light. Makes me happy.

    Got some 2'' pvc pipe, you will see why later. As you can see I cut the rear of the housing out, and cut on the sides to allow the projector to sit correctly and sit lower.




    Rear view


    This is how it will sit. On top of that pvc im putting a ABS cover just like my first attempt. That way the projector has something to mount to. The pvc will be plastic welded to the housing.


    Projector clearence, JUST enough


    Here is how the PVC sits. Almost a perfect fit.
