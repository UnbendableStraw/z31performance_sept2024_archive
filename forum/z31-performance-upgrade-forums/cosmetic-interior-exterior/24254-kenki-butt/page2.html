    Great idea Z_Karma! The color pictured is charcoal metallic- the most popular color of all decals I have sold here on Z31P. This is the only mock-up I have done. I just slapped these on and didn't even remove the 300ZX decal underneath as I wanted to test them first. Tomorrow I will do a real test of the final product.

    The bottom stripes measure 21 1/2" wide by 2 1/2" tall and are cut at a 42.5 degree angle, for those who would like to experiment or make their own. They are designed to be installed DIRECTLY flush with the lower taillight assembly. Once they have been placed on the car, take your fingers and gently press the vinyl adhesive down into the groove at the bottom of the taillight. This insures that there is no unwanted paint visibility. Once installed, the visible portion of the stripe is 2 1/4" wide. This set will come with the charcoal stripes, black pinstriping and a "300ZX" or "300ZX TURBO" decal in the color of your choice, to be placed in the stock position.
