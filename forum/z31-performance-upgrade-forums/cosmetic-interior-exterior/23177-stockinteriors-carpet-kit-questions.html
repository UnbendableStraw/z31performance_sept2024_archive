    Stock interiors will send you free samples, I chose one that closely matched the carpet under the seats since it was the least faded. I dont remeber the number code. Iordered a shade darker anyway so it wasnt really an issue.

    I also ordered mine with the added mass backing....It was pretty thick and I think it gave me a bit of a problem at the back corners where some of the carpet edgeing didnt quite fit back under the plastic moulding.......But its not a show car and it isnt really visible. And the new carpet is WAY nicer than the old stinky stuff that was in the car when I got it. Of course with my exhaust my car is pretty loud anyway, so I really dont know how much sound deadening its really doing.

    The box is pretty large. might fit in your gf's car but if not.....take it out and stuff it in the back seat.....its preformed and you need to let it lie flat and relax before installation in a warm place.....so it wont make a difference, it comes rolled up anyway.
