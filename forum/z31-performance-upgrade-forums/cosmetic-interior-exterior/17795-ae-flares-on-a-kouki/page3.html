    Sorry for reopening an old thread. But pictures of Z31's from Japan (As Dwayne posted) can't be used to determine what type of fenders it has/had. If you look at the Z31club picture Dwayne posted, it came from a Japanese Z31 club headed by Little K and is a picture of an 87 200ZR (member 408). If you look at all the Kouki front end 200ZR's, many have the Zenki fenders. Especially the 87's. I was mainly looking at 200ZR's. The Kouki AE pictured above may have been one of these Zenki bodied Kouki front and rear end Z31's 200ZR's.

    In the JDM, you can't assume there are Kouki rear quarters under Dwayne's attachment. So we don't know the AE flare fitment on a Kouki AE unless we know what was originally under the flares.
