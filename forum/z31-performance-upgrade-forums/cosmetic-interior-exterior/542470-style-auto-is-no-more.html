    Style Auto is no more

    02-17-2019, 05:33 PM
    I'm sure we all know of http://www.styleauto.de/ It was a German company/website that had aftermarket body kits for certain cars, including the Z31 including with front/rear bumpers and side skirts (both zenki & kouki) as well as a rear spoiler. I was informed a few days ago about the owner, Michael Neuman.

    styleauto.jpg

    I first met Michael in person in 2015 (by then, an elderly man) when I stopped by their estate and then again 2017. He showed me around his shop as well as the different molds he had. At the time, he was handcrafting a vintage Porsche racer body for someone. He was in the race industry for 40 years.

    A drive-up clip
    https://drive.google.com/file/d/15DK…ew?usp=sharing

    As his wife mentioned, the molds are available for pickup. As I was just in Germany in September & October, I probably won't be going back until this fall (September/October). Just putting the news out there.
