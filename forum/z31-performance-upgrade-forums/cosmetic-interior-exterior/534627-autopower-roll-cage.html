    I have this in the Shiro

    Thoughts
    -- Hoop fits in nice and tight to everything with full interior. The bolt in piece is pretty easy, granted you still need to tear apart your interior to get the interior pieces off the wheel wells (enjoy that puzzle). I found that cutting the trim pieces with dremmel and using door edging provided a nice clean look. Ensure there are no 90* cuts and heat up the door edging when putting it on --- hell you can just come over and we can do it at my place.

    My bar did not come with harness portion so I had one welded in. It is not offset like the picture --- which I had no issues with OEM shiro Recaro. You could sit comfortably with no restrictions to the drop bar or the harness bar. If anything my setup would be more restricting --- pics below will show

    The finish from autopower is pretty terrible. I would plan on getting it powder coated. It also does not come with hardware. Welds are functional but not super pretty -- hence the wrinkle finish powder coat.
