    The Auto Combine Green Cove Springs FL

    01-07-2015, 01:35 AM
    You Florida guys need to consider coming to this. I've been to all of them, the last event brought in 1200 cars, only 2 of which were Z31s.... We need a bigger showing! There are tons of awesome cars to see, good food, vendors etc. I'm hoping to have the Shiro back up and running in time to make it myself.

    https://www.facebook.com/events/371870366307223/

    For you non Facebook folks.

    Welcome to The Auto Combine 2015!

    Just a little background on The Combine -
    - We held our first and only Combine to date in February of 2012.
    - We had 945 cars pass through the gate.
    - Second event was August 2014 and we had 1,200 cars.
    - Our venue has nearly 6 acres of concrete parking and a 20,000 square foot air plane hangar to park in. Shoot it may be more space than that but we'd rather aim low than high.
    - Big thanks to the blogs and vendors who came to the first and second ones and have really pushed us to continue holding Auto Combines.

    So now that you know a little history, let's talk about 2015.

    Date - March 15th, 2015.

    Official event hours are from 11 - 4. Each year there has been an influx of people who do photo shoots on the property after the event, so we do leave the gates open until 6 so you have time to snap some good pictures. Over the last two events, their has been 3 magazine spreads to come from shoots on our property and a dozen major blog articles. We do expect people to start lining up early to get a good spot. In 2014 we ended up opening the gates around 10:15 to decrease traffic blockage. Please line up in two single file lines. That way we can get everyone in as quick as possible.

    Address - 965 Leonard C Taylor Pkwy
    Green Cove Springs, FL 32043.

    Aka SIK Creation's back yard.

    We will have flags at the main road to help people locate the event. Though we don't see any problems in people trying to find the venue, it's rather large.

    Admission - $5 per person.

    Vehicles with 3 or more passengers will only be $10.

    There will be plenty of vendors at this years event. From stickers to car parts to apparel, there will be a vendor for it all.

    So far we've locked down the following vendors and sponsors
    - Lowered Lifestyle (Coming from Orlando, FL)
    - SLAMMEDEnuff? (Coming from Memphis, TN)
    - SOUTHRNFRESH (Coming from Atlanta, GA)
    - Identity Design (Jacksonville Native)
    - FUNKfashion (Jacksonville Native)
    - VAPExMASTERS (Jacksonville Native)
    - ODB Welding Werks (Jacksonville Native)
    - SIK Creations (Jacksonville Native)
    - fiveninedesign (Jacksonville Native)
    - I Love Driving Slow. (Coming from Tampa, FL)

    And of course there will be food.
    Smoked Meat Militia - BBQ Galore (Cherry Coke Sauce… nom nom)
    Two Chink's - Lumpia, Rice, and Chicken Sticks (Yes that's their name lol)

    Official vendor sign ups begin on January 1st, If you're interested, please shoot us an email at theautocombine@gmail.com. We are limiting the amount of vendors we have for each category of products sold, so please contact us sooner than later or we can't promise availability.

    There will be music of course! Last year we had J.Stahr rocking inside the hangar but we're changing it up for 2015. To have more room for vendors and booth cars, we're moving J.Stahr plus special guest djs outside across from the hangar.

    If you have any questions, please post on the event page wall or shoot us a message directly to our fan page. Any large clubs looking to come out and want to reserve spots, please contact us and we can make it happen.

    Now let the waiting begin!
