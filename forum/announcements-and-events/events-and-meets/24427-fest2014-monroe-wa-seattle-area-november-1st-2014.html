    Hey guys - just wanted to give everyone a heads up on this really neat event in the PNW!
    Acadiana Sports Car Orphanage will be there with a booth displaying all sorts of goodies.

    We will have two confirmed Z31s in display inside the building as well.
    Come on out and take a look at some fantastic nostalgic iron!

    Saturday, November 1
    at 5:00pm - 10:00pm in PDT
    Monroe Fair Grounds
    14405 179th Ave SE, Monroe, Washington 98272


    Your invited to the largest INDOOR event Happenstance events has called #FEST . Fest is a festival of all things automotive. From classics to the latest models showcasing fabulous work.

    This all new Expo center next to the raceway is heated and amazing. We should be able to fit 80 cars inside and 150 outside. There is a large barn for the swapmeet folks to sell or browse for goodies.

    We will feature Old School European with Nostalgic JDM all on one floor all together like an event you have never been to before. Next to the show area is a large building stuffed full of classic rusty chrome parts for your build.

    Arm bands will be worn at all times of the event.
    Children under 12 are always FREE
    Outside Lot parking is FREE
    VIP parking is available for a fee
