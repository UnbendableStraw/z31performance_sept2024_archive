    Some recommendations for 1st timers .. at the MSA location.

    In the past .. participants were lining up to get entry to the show at 5AM .. that's at least 2-3 hours before official roll-in time. I know, because I was there at 5AM and there were cars in-front of me.

    Make sure you bring your paperwork (reciepts/registration, etc..) Check in goes relatively fast, but you want to have your paperwork on-hand, in-case there are any issues.

    Unless you're a club (and have prior arrangments) .. if you're attending as a group .. you need to roll-in together (this way you'll park together). Note I"m not sure if they'll separate the cars by models or just allow mixed parking.
    The street venue isn't as large as the park .. so it won't cover as large an area ..

    You also want to arrive early to be able to park/stage near the store front .. where all the action typically occurs.
