    ROLL CALL! - The Mitty 2012 - Road Atlanta - April 27-29

    03-31-2012, 11:25 AM


    Due to some issues that arose at work with scheduling that will prevent me from attending BZF, I decided to make the most of the situation by attending another event I love to go to!

    The Classic Motorsports Speedfest at the Mitty is a classic car event held annually at the Road Atlanta race track in Braselton, Georgia. Since the inaugural event in 1978 it has grown to become one of the largest classic car events on the East Coast of the United States.

    Every year classic sports car and racing enthusiasts gather in the hilly country near Atlanta, Georgia, to celebrate the full spectrum of historical and contemporary motorsports. Road Atlanta has been home to the Classic Motorsports Mitty since its inception in 1978, and there's no better place or time to enjoy a motorsports daydream. Join us April 27-29 for a long weekend of action both on and off the track. Even if you're not a competitor, you can get your car on this internationally famous racing circuit by participating in one of the weekend's parade laps.

    Attendance should be in the 10,000-15,000 person range throughout the weekend, and this is simply an incredible experience.
    Getting to watch HSR vintage racing cars blasting through one of the most scenic tracks in the country is something I imagine everyone on this forum would be in awe of.

    ---------------------------------------------------------------------

    Acadiana Sports Car Orphanage will be a vendor at the event, and we will forgoing the amenities of hotel living by camping on the infield in the evenings. I believe there is going to be at least 6-7 Z31P members who will be attending that I am aware of, and the Z Club of Georgia always has a spectacular turnout for this event as well.

    We will be using a 20x10 tent on vendors row, and should have a special guest car making an appearance that should have plenty of installed ASCO goodies for people to look at.

    It would be great to meet everyone, and to get to talk cars, see cars, and enjoy cars for a weekend!
