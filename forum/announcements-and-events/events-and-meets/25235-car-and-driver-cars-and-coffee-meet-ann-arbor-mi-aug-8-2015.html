    Car and Driver Cars and Coffee meet. Ann Arbor, MI Aug 8 2015

    08-03-2015, 04:24 PM
    "Save the date: Saturday, August 8th 2015 we'll be hosting our 2015 summer edition of Cars and Coffee! More info to come so check back for details.

    In keeping with tradition, we will have donuts from Washtenaw Dairy, fresh local brew, and a plethora of metal to drool over. New this year, we'll have a Car and Driver pop-up store where you can purchase all your favorite C/D gear.

    If you would like to coordinate a section where your car club can park together, please contact me directly: jfeeny@hearst.com"


    Here's a link to the FB page! https://www.facebook.com/events/1615534585356688/

    I brought my 85NA out last year, and there was a sexy red kouki outside of the show for a while. Sorry this is short notice, I totally forgot this section of the forum existed lol. Last year you got some free donuts, a free shirt, free mug, and some stickerz. Tons of great cars, and some really interesting people to talk to. As far as I know, there will be a food truck too this year.
