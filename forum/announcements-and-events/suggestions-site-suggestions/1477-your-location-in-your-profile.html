    Today, I was playing around on the Members List … checking out
    members web sites and where they are from.

    I sorted on "Location" to see who else might be from the Norcal
    Bay Area and was amazed at how many don't even list the state
    they are from. Why's that? Are people afraid that someone would

    So to make it easier to find me I re-listed my location as "California, San Leandro USA". That way when the list is sorted on location, my usermane
    is listed with the other Californians. This is something others might think
    about doing.
