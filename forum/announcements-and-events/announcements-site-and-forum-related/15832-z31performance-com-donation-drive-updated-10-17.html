    There's a first time for everything.


    Why: To pay the hosting fees and upgrade to a dedicated server for better performance and more features. We currently have limitations due to the inexpensive shared hosting service the forum runs on.

    What are the advantages?
    - INSANE bandwidth
    - Better performance, easily upgradable as time goes on.
    - Limitless database size (current limit is 500MB, which has been exceeded a few times now).
    - Open-access for viewing the forum without logging in.
    - Specs: 24/7 managed managed server, 300gb/month bandwidth. 2gb ram, 1.5GHz processor.
