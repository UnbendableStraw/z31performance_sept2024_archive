    Mike P perma ban.

    07-13-2014, 02:59 PM
    As everyone probably knows by now, Mike P has been permanently banned from this forum. If you have questions regarding this decision take it up with Mike P or the mods. Mike has been a contributor for a long time on this forum, but has not been getting along with the moderator staff (current and past) for some time. While it is true that Zach and I own this forum, we do very little moderating as this isn't something we have time for. People pass judgment and say that we do "nothing," but they don't live in our world. My life offline is my immediate concern and secondly is my z family whom I have gotten to know very well over the years. I even have met Mike P in person and I know he is a good guy. He is someone I can call my friend, but not all of our friends get along with each other. Like I told Mike P, the issues are between him and the moderating staff. If by proxy, you want to place the blame on us due to the decision of the moderators we have chosen, that's fine. Mike knows that he and I will continue to talk, be friends, and exchange ideas. That's what the community is about!

    So, is this a post about me putting the blame on the moderating staff? NO! This is a post about everyone figuring out how to get along and sharing our love for the z31 chassis. I have urged Mike P to go and start his own forum where he can spread the z31 gospel and not have to worry about ruffling the feathers of our current moderating staff. I think our moderators do a fantastic job, as a matter of fact we get fewer complaints than ever and the moderator section isn't near as active. Only a select few have issues with the moderating staff and were hearing plenty from those folks but I stand behind the moderating staff we have in place. If you have interest in being a mod yourself or have issues with a particular mod; contact any of the admins such as myself, Amreboot, or Zachary Ard.

    Thank You!
