    Site Outage and Site Change

    10-09-2013, 12:00 PM
    All

    There was very large Exploit that VB acknowledged on the 7th/8th. Because of this, our site had been hacked and injected with hundreds of entries of malicious websites.
    In order to protect our user's private information, I brought the site down.
    I spent several hours trying to clean the injection to no success.
    I worked for several more hours upgrading to the latest version of VBulletin.
    Doing this addressed the security issue and repaired the database.

    Its up and running now. But I obviously have more work and clean up to do.
    Please be patient while I am doing this.
    In the meantime, the forum is functional again.

    Thanks!
    Alex
