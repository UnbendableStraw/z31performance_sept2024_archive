    WOW you guys, just WOW. Sorry I have not updated the total in a few days, but some bad news and some GOOD NEWS. I was just adding up the donations and we have exceeded the goal. In under a week, we raised over $1150 from donations!

    The bad news is that after I had my eyes set on a VPS (Virtual Private Server), I started comparison shopping and ended up talking with sales personnel at three other hosting companies. I was told what the first company said the exact opposite of: That a VPS soultion won't offer good performance for a PHPBB3 forum with a large SQL database. :-( Could I make a bigger frown face here?
    Anyway, I confirmed this with a very db-savvy aquaintence in the IT department at work; when using features like search are being used to query the database, page load times and everything else would virtually (no pun intended) come grinding to a hault momentarily. This means one person bombarding the "view new posts" could massively slow down the forum for everyone. We can't have that, so dedicated server option it is. The problem is that's a lot more money… the benefit of a few hosting packages I'm looking at is that they offer tech support WAY beyond simply fixing issues. They can deliver customized forum layouts, install MODs, update software and ensure the best available security (so we won't ever get hacked like what happened ~4 years ago when we were running phpbb2). There is also a 100% uptime promise to go along with that…

    Personally I am leaning towards wanting to improve the forum in every way possible, even if it means spending a little more money. Just to throw it out there… I am willing to put up more out-of-pocket expence in complete transparency (IE you'll all know the costs) if it's desired. It's also time to get the forum sponsors to renew. At the very least we should have AT LEAST year paid for at this point, and I will be taking this "donation drive" global announcement down this week.
