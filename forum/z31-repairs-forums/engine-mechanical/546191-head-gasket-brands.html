    Mahle 5826 is what is generally recommended. I've used them and had no problems.
    They may be stamped STONE or ISHINO but are the same, just rebranded.

    Victor Reinz is also a preferred brand but in looking at the specs, their bore size is listed as 3.472 whereas Mahle is 3.57. Interesting
