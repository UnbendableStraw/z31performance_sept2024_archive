    If you are sick of getting wet or sick of all the little tricks to stop the leaks. Want to restore the car to its original state when it was brand new?

    Well you and I both know the On body weather stripping is expensive, at $298 each! through courtesy Nissan and over $350 elsewhere not many can afford to replace them.

    Now what I have done is went directly to the manufacturer Metro Moulded Parts Inc which work with Car clubs to make reproductions of existing rubber seals etc.

    I showed them the target price we are looking for, they make exact replicas.

    They also support car shows and events with goody bags etc… So if they take on this venture we need to show them there is a demand for this item. Also, once I speak the the new products manager on the phone I will ask for items like their banners etc so we can display at events. I would most likely set it up as free advertisement for them. Kind of like "you listen to us, we spread your name".

    The key here is demand! and people who are serious about restoration and sick of leaks.
