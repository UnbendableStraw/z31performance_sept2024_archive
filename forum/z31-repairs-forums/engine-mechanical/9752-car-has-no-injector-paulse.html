    When I did the auto swap my car wouldnt start at all, meaning no signal to the starter solenoid. I wonder if you blew a fusible link to the injector harness, I have seen it before and it sounds likely to be the case. The fusible links are located in front of the battery in a little black box, there should be three sets of them. I believe its the set that says EGI INJ and EGI CONT should be able to jumper them with similar gauge wire to test whether or not they're burnt. peace.
