    you can change all the bearings with the motor in the car. cross member will have to be removed, as will the oil pan obv. take all the drive belts off. support the front of the crank with a screw jack. take down the main bearing girdle, and use a small screw driver to push the bearing out of the upper journal. slide the new one in, making sure the notch is correct. same thing with the rod bearings.
