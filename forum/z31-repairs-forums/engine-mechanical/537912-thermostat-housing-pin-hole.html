    That isn't worth JB weld. That is corroded AF.

    At the back end of the motor, there is a single 10mm bolt that holds that pipe to the block, near a flange that goes to a section of pipe that feeds the heater core.
    If you can access and remove that, you won't have to remove the plenums.

    If you can't get one from a local parts yard, I'd try to source one from one of the facebook z31 groups. They are way more active than the forum.
