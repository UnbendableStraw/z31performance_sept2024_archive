    if the vacuum line smells like gas then there's a good chance that the pressure regulator is fubar. A way to make sure it is leaking is to disconnect the vacuum line from the intake manifold, plug the port on the manifold, and see if you get fuel coming out of the vacuum line from the pressure reg. One question I do have is for your FPR (pressure reg) do you have it going through the solenoid on the driver's side fender well or is it directly connected to the intake manifold?
