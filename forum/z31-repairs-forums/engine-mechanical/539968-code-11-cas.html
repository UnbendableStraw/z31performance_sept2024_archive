    Might want to work backwards from there. Verify support things are getting power, since you replaced components. If you look at the schematic,
    that system is powered through the EFI relay and protected by a fusible link.

    http://www.xenonzcar.com/z31/z31fsm.php

    Unfortunately it could be a combination of things since you have a custom setup, or it might possibly be a loose fuse:

    https://z31performance.com/forum/z31…-problem/page2
    Check to make sure that your Engine management fuse in your fuse box isn't loose. If it is it won't send a signal to your coil which won't allow your car to start. Because that's what was wrong with mine when it acted like what you where describing. Had to follow the wires back from the distributor to the fuse box and found out that one of the connectors for that fuse wasn't making a good connection.
