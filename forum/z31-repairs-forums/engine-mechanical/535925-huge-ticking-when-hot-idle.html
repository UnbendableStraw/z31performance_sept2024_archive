    Are you sure it's not an exhaust leak due to a busted manifold stud, or cracked manifold.? They can sometimes tick and mimic a lifter noise.

    You could try and run some SeaFoam additive in the oil just before a oil change to help dissolve any gunk in the lifters.
    However the side effect of doing this means that you may be dissolving anything that is keeping a already weak seal from sealing anymore.
    Disclaimer, i've not done this myself, i get a startup tick that goes away pretty quickly.

    If the solvent helps, you might consider switching to synthetic oil if you haven't already done so.
