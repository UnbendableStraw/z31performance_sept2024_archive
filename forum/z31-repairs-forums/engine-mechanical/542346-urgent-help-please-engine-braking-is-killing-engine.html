    84-85 z31's have a lean start issue that is inherent to the ECU. In my experience with my 84T, When it is cold it acts restricted and will misfire if accelerate from a stop and i don't keep the RPM's up. Once the digital temp gauge gets to the 3rd bar, it's like a switch is flipped somewhere in the ECU and it suddenly runs fine. I've tried replacing the CHTS and harness and the old mod where you switched the CHTS signal to the FTS, but it didn't alleviate the issue.

    84-85 ECUs (all) have been reported to have a small lean surge problem during the first 30 seconds or so of engine warm up. This is most likely an issue with the cold-start enrichment and can be remedied by switching from CHTS to FTS signal during startup.
    http://www.xenonzcar.com/z31/ECUdifferences.html

    There was a service bullitin that addressed this where they added some helper module beneath the battery tray. My 84T has this, but still has the same issues. The agreed upon fix for this is to run a newer ECU that is compatible with your particular car.

    http://redz31.suomiz.net/pages/fuel.html
