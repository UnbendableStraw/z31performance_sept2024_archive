    There's no proven power gains. It just gives you more sensible pipe routing, especially for big pipes, if you have an intercooler and/or a relocated turbo. But it's only "bolt on" in the sense that it bolts directly to the LIM, lol. You need to relocate a bunch of things and clearance a bunch more and come up with a couple of custom solutions for stuff. It does make some things easier to get at but compared to getting it to work in the first place, not worthwhile lol. Unless you really specifically want shorter intercooler piping, no, it's not worth it. I'm keeping mine that way because it's done, but like I said, wouldn't do it again.
