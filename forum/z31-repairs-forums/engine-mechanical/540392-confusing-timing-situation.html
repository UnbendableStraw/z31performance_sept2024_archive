    I second what JSM said… there has to be something off about that crank pulley. The camshafts themselves have a guide pin, so you cannot install the sprockets improperly unless you flip them over and install them backwards. If your two cam marks and your crank mark line up, then you're golden. I'd look into getting a different crank pulley, personally. You'll need to change crank pulleys anyway if you plan on running a true Turbo setup… unless you want to swap pulleys on your alternator.
