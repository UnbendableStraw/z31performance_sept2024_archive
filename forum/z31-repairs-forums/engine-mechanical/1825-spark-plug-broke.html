    You will probably have to tap the threads to cut out the left over junk and make sure the threads are clean. Re-cutting the threads to get the plug remnants out is one thing. Getting all the metal pieces out of the cylinder is another.

    I have a long reach magnet that has a U-joint near the end that would fit down through the plug hole. Something like that should get most of the metal shards out, but I would definitely take plenty of time making sure I was dang sure I had that cylinder free of debris.
