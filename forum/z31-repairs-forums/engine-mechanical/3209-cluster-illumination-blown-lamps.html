    Here's an option I threw together and it works very well. You need two packs of DE3021 Lamps, a little wire, solder, and of course a soldering iron. I cut a small piece of wire to go through the holes on the left and right of the stock rubber piece, I bent the wire over the back pole and soldered the lamp to the wire and the wire going through the back pole to the contact on the back side. The new lamps are a little bit larger than the old set, but it fits just fine, no modifications were necessary. This could be cleaned up a little with real clamps so the bulbs could be changed more easily in the future.
