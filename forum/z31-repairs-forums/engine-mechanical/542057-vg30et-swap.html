    I had the same exact problem when I did my NA2T swap on my '85. I used the factory NA '85 harness, and had an '84 Turbo ECU. It wouldn't power up the EFI relay, nor the fuel pump relay. It is more than likely your ECU. Also, make sure you are running the correct O2 sensor for your harness and ECU combination. The '84-'85 Turbos ran a different O2 sensor than the '86. Just a heads up.
