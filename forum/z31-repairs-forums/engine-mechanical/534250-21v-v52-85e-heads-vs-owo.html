    Well, they did all come on different models/cars but there aren't any real major changes to the heads like your chevy example. 21V are the early A/B heads. The V52 heads added the extra water passages for the W engine but are otherwise identical to the 21Vs as far as I can tell. The OWO heads from the 3.3 are a much cleaner casting and appear to have higher/more floor on the intake port. They also have 10mm exhaust studs. The 85E heads came on the Maximas (others maybe?) and I've heard are the same casting as the OWO, but I have no first hand knowledge to verify that claim.
