    Its not difficult to replace the shocks on the Z31. Gabriel shocks with lower springs.....mmm…not the best combination of spring and shocks to have. The only advantage you have is that Autozone sell the shocks with lifetime warranty, so you won't spend money replacing the shocks. I will recommend you to buy good shocks when you can like Tokico, Bilstein or maybe KYB. Gabriel shocks are sell to use them with stock(OEM) configuration so if you install lower spring they won't last. I believe the guys here will tell you almost the same.
