    The 03-04 350z 6spd transmissions are junk. From 05-09 they are better for sure. I have no personal experience for this, just from what I've read into.
    I have also read that some boosted 350z owners switch down to the z32 r30a transmission.

    The z32 r30a transmissions are proven tough and have been pounded on by high horse power builds.
    You can get r30a JDM transmissions new (called 98+ spec).
    They are Nissan redesigned r30a's with stronger synchro's. They are pricey though.
    Take a look on ebay.


    I personally run a z32 r30a in my z31. At 25psi, I dyno'd at 627rwhp. My transmission shifts great, no gear grinding.

    Unless you are pushing past 800rwhp, I don't see a need to go any other transmission.

    Just my $.02.
