    You mean the master cylinder or slave cylinder. Both are made by Nabco/Tokico. aand easily found via Google.

    Master cylinder for turbo and non turbo = 30610-p6500


    https://conceptzperformance.com/niss…00_p_25656.php

    turbo = 30620-48P0A

    https://www.nissanpartsdeal.com/part…620-48p0a.html

    non turbo = 30620-v5011
