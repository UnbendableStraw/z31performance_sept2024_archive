    A seal might have popped and you lost fluid. I've had 2 clutch slave cylinder seals fail when the weather has been at or around freezing for an extended period.

    Check fluid level, look for leaks, replace what is leaking. If no leaks check the pedal mechanism to make sure nothing worked it's way loose.
