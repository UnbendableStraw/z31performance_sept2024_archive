    For anyone looking for exact specs, here they are (for the 2+2):

    Hood Lift:
    Extended Length (inches) 14.62
    Compressed Length (inches) 9.61
    Stroke (inches) 5.01
    Pounds of Force 85
    Shaft Diameter (mm) 10
    Body Diameter (mm) 22
    Shaft Connection Off-Set Blade (.605 dia. hole)
    Body Connection Off-Set Blade (.605 dia. hole)
    Hatch Lift:
    Extended Length (inches) 21.91
    Compressed Length (inches) 14.01
    Stroke (inches) 7.90
    Pounds of Force 56
    Shaft Diameter (mm) 8
    Body Diameter (mm) 19
    Shaft Connection Ball Socket (Nylon) 10mm (was Steel)
    Body Connection Ball Socket (Nylon) 10mm (was Steel)
    * If you're getting universals, only the compressed length, extended length and connection type matters.
