    the Blister fenders you see on Crab's car are for a S30. there are none like that "For" a Z31, like most things. to suit the look, needs to be appropriately low, and the wheel/tire combo needs to suit it as well. with that being the case, you cant be afraid to cut, trim, bend, drill your body work on your car.

    Personally I prefer the Zenki flares as they are, shape wise, and like to find a setup that suits them well. I go back and forth on the fender mirror and blister fenders sometimes too, but it's one of those things where, if you REALLY want it, you have to REALLY be willing to go through with it, and stick to it.
