    I thought I would weigh in on this. I contacted my Nissan parts guy to find what OEM Z31 center console lids are still available. Below are the part numbers, along with their color and availability (as of 5-8-15):

    96920-21P00 ("G" code - gray) - No Longer Available
    96920-21P01 ("C" code - brown/tan) - 1 in the US; 24 in Japan
    96920-21P02 ("A" code - red/burgundy) - 2 in the US; 23 in Japan
    96920-21P03 ("B" code - blue) - No Longer Available
    96920-21P04 ("T" code - black) - 13 in the US

    Current list on all available is $119.30, but discounted to $89.48 at www.factorynissanparts.com
