    keep your car out of the sun, if you can. get a sunshade for the windshield. theZstore offers a very nice UV sun shade that is custom fit for your Z (not the universal type). it is a bit more expensive but its good quality and it will last. for dash covers, I use velour dash carpet matts from www.dashdesigns.com -- good quality, made to fit. another thing you can do is get the hard full face dash cover from theZstore. window tint also helps.

    fortunately for me, my two Z31's had a perfect dash and even in this condition, I still follow the advice above to ensure they stay that way.... and I live in an extremely hot and sunny climate out here in the middle east. again the best thing you can do is keep your Z out of the sun as much as possible.
