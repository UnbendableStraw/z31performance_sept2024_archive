    Water intrusion spare tire well and rear passenger quarter panel

    03-02-2024, 06:32 PM
    Alas my 10 minutes of searching the forum have come up fruitless, even though I know I've seen posts on this before. Ran the Z through the car wash today after removing all the seats (save the driver one) and removing the carpet. Purpose was to clean under the carpet before installing the new carpet and… leak check the car. I was noticing some dampness in the rear passenger quarter panel with quite a bit of mud before hand. While washing, I noticed the driver side T-top is still leaking at the front. Should be a simple fix of more silicone. However, there's a little drip into the spare tire well as well as the water inside the passenger rear quarter (next to the well). I dont' believe it's the rear quarter window-- there's no evidence of that, plus the butyl tape is new and looks to be in position. Where else couild the leak(s) be coming from? Side marker light? hatch rubber?

    Ther rear tail lights commonly have sagging butyl issues too, which can let water into the spare tire well.

    I had a pesky leak in my 84T that the water was coming in from the rear t-top drain (the seam seal had cracked) and instead of it leaking straight down, it ran down inside the hatch pillar (c pillar ?) and dripped down onto the trim before running into the rear quarter panel area. That was a pain in the ass to determine.
