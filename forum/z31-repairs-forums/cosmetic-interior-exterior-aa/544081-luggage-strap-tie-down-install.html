    I'm going to assume you still have the straps installed along with the other two anchors. Strap loops through the anchor you have pictured then secures to the cleat. There is a v-shaped notch in the trunk (center of the trunk on the edge close to the lights) that the pin (silver thing in the pic) secures to.
