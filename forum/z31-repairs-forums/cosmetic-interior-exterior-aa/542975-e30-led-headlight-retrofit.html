    Originally posted by jy1235689 View Post

    Problem with koukis are that the housings are Nissan and made of glass so you're SOL in modding them to have halos inside.
    Nope, bake them at 200F for about 20min. The glass lenses are glued to the metal housings with seam sealer and can carefully be separated when hot/soft enough.

    Clean off all the old seam sealer and reassemble with new seam sealer once your halo rings are installed. I just did this with some JDM zenki headlights that are a replaceable 9004 bulb like the kouki housings.
