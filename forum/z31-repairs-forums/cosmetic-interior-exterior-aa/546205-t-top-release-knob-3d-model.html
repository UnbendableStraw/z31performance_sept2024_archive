    I got new, OEM ones from nissanpartsdeal.com. They have both the base and the twist knobs, but only in black. All other colors are marked as discontinued. I’m not sure how long these black ones will last, but they’re listed as in stock as of 12/18/2021.

    Parts numbers for the knobs and base in black as follows-

    Black twist knobs: 73556-16P00

    Black base: 73565-03P00
