    Metro restyling. What you see on the door is vinyl carbon fiber fabric, like a cloth, laid over a 1/8 inch foam backing. The carbon fiber effect in normal light is not any way near as noticeable as when the camera flashes.

    http://www.metrorestyling.com/Metro-…lkcffabric.htm

    This looked so good I decided to experiment with stretchable vinyl sheets. The below pictures are their 3M Di-Noc carbon fiber vinyl sheets. With a hair dryer, you can cover an outside mirror, it stretches so much. I decided to cover the seat back fiber boards with this stuff, and then decided to go for it and cover the lower inside trim of the entire car. This is what I have done so far. Next are the shock tower trim pieces. That will be a challenge with the many curves and shapes. If I don't like what I am doing, you can always pull this stuff off and go back to the original 30 year old plastic.
