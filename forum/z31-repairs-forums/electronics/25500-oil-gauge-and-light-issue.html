Any thoughts as to what my issue is? Thanks!

    The oil sender is a 2 wire unit that uses the engine block as ground.

    One wire is variable resistor based on pressure.

    The other is a low pressure switch. that triggers your low pressure light. This one is normally open so if someway you cut the wire it will trigger the light.


    you might try to temperarily ground this wire to the block with the key in the run position with the engine off to double check your wiring is in one piece. I know my harness has a problem on the sender wire but not the pressure light, hence the aftermarket oil pressure gauge.
