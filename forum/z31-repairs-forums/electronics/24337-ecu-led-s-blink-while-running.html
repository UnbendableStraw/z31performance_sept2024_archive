    Well, I didn't find the explanation of the LED's, but I did solve the bogging problem. It turns out the coil wire to the distributor was not very well seated on the coil end. It was good enough for the engine to crank and idle, but wouldn't support the additional required current to fire at elevated rpm's… the one time it worked properly, I must've accidentally bumped it and seated it better, then bumped it again next time I was in that area of the engine bay.
