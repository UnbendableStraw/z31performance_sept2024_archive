    That small valve when open redirects a small portion of air to the lower vents when you have the vent selector at dash or defrost mode. You'll feel the A/C down there if it's a really hot day and you have shorts on. If it's making noise I would disconnect it and not worry about it. You likely wonn't notice any difference it made if it was working right.

    Look on xenonzcar.com for the owners manual and you'll find the full function description there.
