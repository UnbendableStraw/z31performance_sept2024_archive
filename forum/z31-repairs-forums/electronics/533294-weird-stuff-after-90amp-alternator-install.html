    I just installed the maxima 90amp alternator into my Z and I am getting some weird stuff happening.

    When I step on the brakes, my headlights flicker and my cd player resets. Eyeballing the gauge I can see a huge voltage drop that lasts just a split second when the brakes are applied.

    Anyone have any idea what could be wrong? The alternator seems to be charging fine and it is outputting the proper voltage. Is it possible I screwed something up during the install or it is a new problem entirely? I looked around the rear hatch area to see if there was a loose ground for the brake lights, but did not find anything.

    Alternator was new, not a reman, that I got off rock auto a few years ago… I hope it's not the culprit since I probably can't return it by now.
