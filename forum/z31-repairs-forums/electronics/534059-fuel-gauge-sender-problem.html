    Here in the US there's a 10A fuse that supplies switched power to the gauges. Middle Column, 3rd one down. From there the sender works as variable resistor to ground.

    Basically your gauge is dropping the 7V.

    Its my guess that you are not using a high impedance volt meter and its acting like the sender by providing a path to ground.
