    The only thing i have is the 5pin DIN pinout for the Hitachi CSK-7101ZG (no equalizer)
    CSK-7101ZK 5pin DIN pinout.jpg

    This is from the stereo side. The earlier Z31 amp/stereo combo used chassis ground for the negative.

    If you compare that to this aux amp image for a 2002 Altima (that i found online)
    Aux_input_diagram.jpg

    It might be possible that your audio signals are on the same as the altima (atleast the positive outputs match)
    You should be able to test this by connecting a scrap headphone speaker to those pins (4&6 or 5&7) and listen
    for sound from the headunit.

    A loud pop followed by smoke means that's not it and you shorted 12V across the voice coil, which is why you use a scrap speaker.
