    Sorry as I'm about a year late to the party here but posting for reference if still needed.
    the Automatic climate control units can display either C for Canada or F for US.
    The trick is inside the Automatic climate control on the circuit board.
    They have a jumper wire.
    Sorry I don't have my unit in front of me but basically jumper wire connected = one country temp display
    jumper wire cut = the other countries temp display
