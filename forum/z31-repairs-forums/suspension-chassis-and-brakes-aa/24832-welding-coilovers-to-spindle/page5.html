    There is a difference between kouki and zenki with the angle of the spindle. However its negligible since you have camber adjustments up top and the track width is minimal. There is a difference between NA and turbo tubes, but they are cut low enough its all the same diameter.

    Depending on what dust shields you use you may need to trim or switch rear seals on the hubs. All very easy to do.
