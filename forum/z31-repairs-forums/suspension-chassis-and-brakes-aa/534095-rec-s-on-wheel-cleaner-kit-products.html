    Is it just grime or is your clear coat worn and peeling -- then you need to refinish.

    For maintenance wash I use diluted P21s
    For heavy dirt I go staight to the sonex -- I would use this, agitate with some brushes and a pressure washer
    Occasionally use ironX depending if its heavier brake dust

    I agitate the small spaces with a horse hair or boars hair detail brush
    Wheel woolies for mesh
    For the inner barrel I use the long narrow wheel brushes

    Scrub the tire with stiff bristle brush

    I seal with Chemical Guys wheel guard
    I dress the tires with Chemical Guys VRP
