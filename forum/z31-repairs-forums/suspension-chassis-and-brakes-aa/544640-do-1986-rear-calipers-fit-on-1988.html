    We're gonna go the "teach you to fish" route…

    Easy:

    On RockAuto:

    look up calipers for 88
    scroll down
    look up calipers for 86

    compare part numbers between the two (also application date range is usually shown when you click on the part number)

    if they are the same = interchangeable
    if they are different = no interchange

    Or:

    Go to nissanpartsdeal

    look up same info on parts diagrams they have for each model.


    You can also do this on autoparts store websites like NAPA, Oreilly, etc,,,

    No problem, thanks for learning

    They aren't interchangeable because the hub on the 87-89 rear control arms is positioned outward 10mm further than the 84-86.

    The 84-86 caliper would not be center aligned to the disc when mounted to the 87-89 control arm bracket.

    You could shim it out 10mm if you absolutely had to, and had a source for the shims.
