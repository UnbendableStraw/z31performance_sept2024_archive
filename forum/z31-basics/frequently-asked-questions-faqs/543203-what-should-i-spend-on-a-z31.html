    It looks incredible because they paid someone to detail it. Hit them at 4500. The 84's and 85's have lean start issues with the ECU that Nissan never fixed correctly. Look at the valve covers and see if the fuel injector wiring is marked with yellow tape. If it is, then the Injector service campaign has been completed. Seat reupholstery can be 350+ Look under the rear carpet and the tool kit on the left for rust. These cars leak water eventually through the tail light and rear side window butyl seals. Also, at that age and mileage, it is just ripe for a bunch of things (usually rubber seals/bushings) to need replacing.
