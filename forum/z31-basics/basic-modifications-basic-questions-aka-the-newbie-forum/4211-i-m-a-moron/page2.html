    Re: I'm a moron



    So now that we know how many lbs/min this turbo flows at 15 PSI or a PR of 2.02. And you probably want to hit full boost by half of your red line so 3250 right?

    You have to do the CFM equation of the engine for 3250 and 6500.
    - - - - Math Time - - - -
    295.5 CFM @ 3250 and 590 CFM @ 6500.

    Now you have to convert CFM into Lbs/Min. This is done by dividing CFM by 14.27.

    295.5/14.27=20.7 Lbs/min 590/14.27= 41.34 Lbs/min

    So now you have the 3 Magic numbers.

    1 - Your PR of 2.02, which is the Left side of the Turbo Map.

    2 & 3 - Your Range of full boost action from 20.7-41.34, which is the lower numbers on the Turbo Map.

    If you go outside of those circles to the left, you have gone past the surge Limit. That is bad. Now it is your turn. Just match the numbers up and see if they fall into the map. So tell me, does it seem to work?
