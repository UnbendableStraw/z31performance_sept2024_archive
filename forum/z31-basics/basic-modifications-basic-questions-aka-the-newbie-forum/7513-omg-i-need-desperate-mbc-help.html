    Um, I highly doubt it is the pop off valve causing you to only hit 5 psi of boost. Why would the pop off valve allow him to hit 6psi in stock form but then knock it down to 5 when he slaps the MBC on there? You probably have your boost controller hooked up wrong.
    Pull off the boost controller, look at it, play with it, touch it squeeze, hold it, love it and figure out how it works. Remember, you are trying to manipulate the signal to the waste gate actuator, essentially blocking air from getting to it.

    For your gauge placement. You should put it on your plenum, this way you know what the ENGINE is seeing, not what the pressure is at any other random point or pipe.
