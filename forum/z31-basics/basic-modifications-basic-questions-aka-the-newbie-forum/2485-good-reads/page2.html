    A few great books I have.
    "Power Secrets" by Smokey Yunick
    Very good information based on his racing experience.
    "Supercharging, Turbocharging, & Nitrous Oxide" by Earl Davis and Diane Perkins-Davis
    Less info then "Maximum Boost" but still a good read.
    "How to build high-performance ignition systems" by Todd Ryden
    Good info on the principals of all styles of ignition systems.
    "How to build Horsepower Volume 1" by David Vizard.
    Similar information as "Power Secrets" but still good.
