    That is correct

    s13 has the room to run it under as the subframe allows.

    z31 just doesnt have the spacing to do so. I have both the MSA and CM exhaust, and the MSA was an easier fit with the assembly. Occasionally MSA will run a sale on the 3" version for $159 for which I picked up my years back. I see it pop up a few times since then. I have not seen their 'non turbo' deal but the piping looks to be nice (questionable muffler).

    Other option would be to go with oval or square narrow for clearance under the axle but not sure that would be that much better.

    Yes, they are everywhere. It just depends on what you are looking for - for nice mandrel pieces performance shops will do a much better job.

    For the s13 subframe, I have a fully custom exhaust. I relocated my muffler to where the cat is located (3" in 3" out), where the OEM muffler is located I went with y pipe with dual 2.5" out..

    CM and MSA have the hard work figured out with over axle, which will give shops some difficulty, where cost end up evening out between full custom and pre made.

    Having had both, I would probably go full custom again.
