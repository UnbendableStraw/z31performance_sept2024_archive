    1.Unreliable
    2.Crappy "EFI" (throttle body injection, though) that is often problematic
    3.Weak engine…2.6L Inline 4 with a small turbo, crappy head…weak internals
    4.Worse suspension geometry than the Z31
    5.Weak drivetrain
    6.Difficult to source parts for, new and used
    7.Slow unless swapped
