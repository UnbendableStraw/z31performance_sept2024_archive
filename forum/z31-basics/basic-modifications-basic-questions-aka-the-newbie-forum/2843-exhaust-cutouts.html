    I dont understand the cutout by the muffler thing.

    If I were to do a cut out it would be for the simple fact that I would be able to the best kind of exhaust for a turbo car (which is NO exhaust). Therefore MY cutout would be placed right after the turbocharger. If I am running a cutout then it is not for the sound, it is for the advantage of NOT having exhaust pluming as a restriction. This would also save me however much on a custom exhaust that I would no longer need, if I could just flip a switch and open up my electric cutout.
